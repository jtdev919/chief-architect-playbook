# Capability Map Example (Template)

## Purpose
Create a shared language between business and technology to align investments.

## Level 1 Capabilities (Example)
- Customer Management
- Order Management
- Product & Catalog
- Billing & Payments
- Fulfillment & Logistics
- Marketing & Growth
- Data & Analytics
- Identity & Access
- Observability & Operations

## Level 2 Example: Order Management
- Order Capture
- Order Validation
- Pricing & Promotions
- Inventory Allocation
- Order Orchestration
- Returns / Refunds
- Notifications

## Mapping
For each capability track:
- System of record
- Supporting systems
- Integration touchpoints
- Data ownership
- Pain points & risks
- Modernization priority

## Output
A capability map becomes the foundation for:
- Platform roadmap
- Application rationalization
- Integration strategy
- Data governance scope
