# Architecture Review Board (ARB)

## Purpose
The Architecture Review Board exists to ensure architectural consistency,
long-term platform health, and alignment with business strategy.

## Scope
- Major platform decisions
- Cross-domain integrations
- Technology introductions
- Significant architectural deviations

## Principles
- Guardrails over gatekeeping
- Decisions documented, not debated repeatedly
- Exceptions allowed with explicit tradeoffs

## Outputs
- Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)
- Approved reference architectures
- Exception tracking
